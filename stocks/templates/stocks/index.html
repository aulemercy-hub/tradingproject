F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What is TradeLens and who is it for?

TradeLens is a market analysis and trading insight platform designed for beginner, intermediate, and professional stock traders looking to make smarter, data-driven decisions.

Do I need prior trading experience to use TradeLens?

No. TradeLens is beginner-friendly and includes educational tools, simple charts, and guided insights to help new traders learn confidently.

What markets does TradeLens support?

TradeLens focuses on stock market analysis, with tools for tracking price movements, trends, and key technical indicators.

Can I cancel or change my subscription anytime?

Yes. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time—no long-term commitments required.

Is TradeLens real-time or delayed data?

Free users get delayed market data, while paid plans provide real-time pricing, alerts, and advanced analytics.

Is my data secure on TradeLens?

Absolutely. TradeLens uses secure encryption and industry best practices to protect your data and account information.
